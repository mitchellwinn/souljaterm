The retro terminal with Roll.

A real shell that runs Claude Code, with Roll living inside it — narrating, alerting, and taking commands while you work.

  • ▸ Real shells running Claude Code in colorful, rainbow-ordered tabs
  • ▸ Hand Roll any job with !do this — she runs it as an agent while you work, every step shown live
  • ▸ Plan-first preview before she touches files, so you always see the task before it runs
  • ▸ She narrates your sessions and pings you the moment Claude needs you
  • ▸ Mute her or tune her volume to taste, anytime
  • ▸ Native on macOS, Windows and Linux — your machine, your Claude login, free
