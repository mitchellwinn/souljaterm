The retro terminal with Roll.

A real shell that runs Claude Code, with Roll living inside it — narrating, alerting, and taking commands while you work.

  • ▸ Real shells running Claude Code in tabs that auto-color themselves by directory
  • ▸ Roll lives in the corner — hand-drawn expressions, lip-synced, reacting to your session in character
  • ▸ Hand her any job with !do this — she plans it, shows you the plan, then runs it as an agent with every step in a live panel
  • ▸ She narrates Claude's work and pings you the instant it needs a human
  • ▸ Her own chiptune voice — Japanese "animalese" or synth blips, plus reaction sound effects, all tunable or mutable
  • ▸ A real CRT shader on her face (you're looking at it) with adjustable scanlines, glow and curvature
  • ▸ Speaks English or 日本語, runs on your own Claude login — native on macOS, Windows & Linux, free
